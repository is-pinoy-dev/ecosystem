## 1. Overview

is-pinoy.dev is a free subdomain service operated by the **is-pinoy-dev** open source community on GitHub. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the **Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)** and its implementing rules.

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a data subject.

## 2. What We Collect

When you register a subdomain, we collect only the information you voluntarily submit via a GitHub pull request:

- Your **GitHub username**
- Your chosen **subdomain name**
- Your **DNS records** (the targets your subdomain points to)
- The **date** of your registration
- Your **email address** *(optional — only if you choose to provide it; if included, it will be **publicly visible** in the repository)*

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or any other personal data beyond what is visible in your GitHub pull request.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

- Provision and manage your subdomain in our DNS configuration
- Identify your registration for support and abuse resolution
- Maintain a public record of registered subdomains in the open source repository
- Contact you regarding your subdomain (only if an email address was provided)

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

## 4. Public Nature of Registrations

All subdomain registrations are made via pull requests to a **public GitHub repository** ([is-pinoy-dev/domains](https://github.com/is-pinoy-dev/domains)). By submitting a registration, you acknowledge that your GitHub username, subdomain name, DNS records, feature flags, and any optional fields you include (such as your email address) will be publicly visible in that repository and its history.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The service relies on the following third-party platforms, each governed by their own privacy policies:

- **GitHub** — pull request and repository hosting ([github.com/privacy](https://github.com/privacy))
- **Cloudflare** — DNS record hosting, traffic proxying (when `"proxied": true` is set on your record), Worker infrastructure for built-in tools, and Analytics Engine for platform visit metrics ([cloudflare.com/privacypolicy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/))

When your DNS record is proxied through Cloudflare, visitor traffic to your subdomain passes through Cloudflare's network. This is subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy, not ours.

## 5a. Built-in Tools and Site Fetching

If you opt in to built-in tools (such as Site Audit), our infrastructure will make server-side HTTP requests to your subdomain to retrieve and analyze its content (HTML, metadata, and response headers). By enabling a tool, you consent to this. No data from your site's responses is stored beyond what is necessary to display results within the tool session.

We have no control over the data practices of third-party platforms listed above.

## 6. Platform Analytics

To monitor platform health and understand aggregate traffic patterns, we passively log visits to proxied subdomains on `*.is-pinoy.dev`.

**What we log per visit:**

- The **subdomain name** (e.g. `juan` from `juan.is-pinoy.dev`)
- The visitor's **country** (ISO 2-letter code, derived from Cloudflare edge metadata — no geolocation lookup is performed and no IP address is stored)
- A **timestamp** (provided automatically by Cloudflare Analytics Engine)

We do **not** log paths, query strings, IP addresses, user-agent strings, referrers, or any other request details at the platform level.

**This logging only applies to proxied subdomains.** If your DNS record has `"proxied": false` (DNS-only mode), your subdomain's traffic goes directly to your origin and is never intercepted by our infrastructure — no visit data is collected for DNS-only subdomains.

Platform traffic requests to `/_tools/*` paths (such as Site Audit) are handled by separate tool workers and are not logged by the platform analytics system.

Visit data is stored in **Cloudflare Analytics Engine** and is subject to [Cloudflare's privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/). Data is used solely for aggregate platform reporting and is not shared, sold, or used for advertising purposes. This processing is carried out in compliance with the **Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173)**.

## 7. Data Retention

Your subdomain record is retained for as long as your subdomain remains active. If your subdomain is removed — by your request or by the maintainers — your DNS record will be deleted from the active configuration. Historical git commits in the public repository may still reference your username as part of the immutable repository history.

## 8. Your Rights

Under RA 10173, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request confirmation of what data we hold about you
- **Correction** — request correction of inaccurate information
- **Erasure** — request removal of your subdomain and associated data
- **Portability** — receive a copy of your data in a readable format
- **Object** — object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, open an issue at [github.com/is-pinoy-dev](https://github.com/is-pinoy-dev) with your request.

## 9. Security

Subdomain records are stored in a public GitHub repository. While we follow responsible open source practices, we cannot guarantee the security of publicly hosted data. Do not submit sensitive personal information. Your GitHub username and any optional fields you include (such as your email address) will be publicly visible.

## 10. Children's Privacy

This service is intended for developers and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Changes will be published in this document and announced via the [is-pinoy-dev GitHub organization](https://github.com/is-pinoy-dev). Continued use of your subdomain after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 12. Contact

For privacy-related concerns, data subject requests, or questions about this policy, open an issue at [github.com/is-pinoy-dev](https://github.com/is-pinoy-dev).
